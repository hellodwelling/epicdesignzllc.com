Our Services

What we are best at - expert body mods & upgrading

Wraps

Wraps allow you to change the vehicle's appearance in a very short period of time and in turn allows you to remove the wrap, returning the vehicle back to its original condition if necessary.

Window Tinting

Tinting prevents fading and damage to your car's interior from ultraviolet light exposure. Tinting can also help to keep the interior of a vehicle cool as well as reduce the glare produced by the headlights of other vehicles.

Sound System Installation

Customize the sound quality of your car and improve the output from the speaker system.

Frame Work

Support your vehicle's mechanical components and body. Let us Deal with static and dynamic loads, without undue deflection or distortion.

Gold & Silver Leafing

Up the look of your vehicle with gold and silver leafing. Give us a call to get your vehicle sparkling in no time!

Fabrication

Metal fabrication makes it possible for you to build machines and structures from raw materials.

Painting

Paint used on automobiles is used for both protection and decoration purposes. We can help with all your painting needs (i.e., candy/pearl/two-tones).
